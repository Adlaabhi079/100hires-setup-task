# Research Notes

## Common Themes Across Experts

1. AI accelerates content production but does not replace strategy.
2. Search intent remains critical for SEO success.
3. Topical authority is more important than publishing isolated articles.
4. Human review is essential for AI-generated content.
5. Internal linking improves content discoverability and rankings.
6. Content distribution is as important as content creation.
7. Keyword research remains a foundation of SEO.
8. High-quality content consistently outperforms low-value AI content.

## Key Learnings

* Successful SEO combines AI efficiency with human expertise.
* Modern content workflows rely on research, optimization, and distribution.
* Content quality and user value remain the most important ranking factors.
