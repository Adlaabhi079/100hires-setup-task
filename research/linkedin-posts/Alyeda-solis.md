Topic:
Bing is releasing New AI Visibility Insights in Bing Webmaster Tools: Intents, Topics, Citation Share, Comparison option

Summary:

Intents, Topics, Citation Share, and Compare are now beginning to roll out in preview within Bing Webmaster Tools globally today.
Thanks Krishna Madhavan and the whole Bing team for being the leaders in providing the AI search data needed by the search community
to better understand and optimize for AI search  Can't wait to use it. 

Key Insights:

* Intents: With the new Intents feature, grounding queries in the AI Performance Report are now classified into broader categories such as Informational, Commercial, Navigational, Learn and Solve, Research, Creation, Local, and more.
 This should help publishers move beyond simply seeing which queries triggered citations and begin understanding the broader query context our systems associate with those citation appearances. 
* Seeing Visibility Through Topics Instead of Individual Queries:  Grouping related grounding queries into broader thematic clusters.
  AI systems reason across concepts and themes rather than isolated keywords. Topics should help publishers understand visibility in the same thematic structure that modern AI systems use to organize information.
